## Computing

- Heterogeneus Computing
  - My entire career has been focused on writing software for CPUs.
  - I have written some SIMD code for learning and have well over a decade of
    experience with CPU pthread style concurrency/parallelism.
  - I have not written a CUDA kernel to do anything useful.
  - I have not written code for any other non-CPU hardware.
  - I do not understand the ISA for CUDA hardware.
  - I do not fully understand how CUDA code is loaded into the GPU and ran.
- Languages
  - I have very little understanding of modern C++.
  - I used C++ in my undergrad but have not had a professional need to use it.
  - I feel there are a lot of features of C++ that are fundamental to computing
    that I have not had sufficient time with.
  - Zig, I want to learn Zig since it is a modern, low-level language with much
    less pain than Rust.
- Compilers
  - I have very little understanding of the internal workings of compilers.

    decisions made about their design.
